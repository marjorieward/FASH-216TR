Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Half Scale Oaktag Slopers
    • 0.5 or 0.7 mm mechanical pencils
    • Erasers (if needed)
    • Gridded or alphanumeric pattern paper
    • Paper Scissors
    • ½ inch wide MATTE/SATIN FINISH scotch tape
    • Clear straight ruler with 1/8 inch markings
    • Pushpins or sewing pins
    • Fashion French curve/Varyform curve

During Class:

  • Dart distribution and the 2-dart bodice sloper.
  • Shoulder and Armhole Princess Stylelines.

After Class:

  • Complete dart rotation, transformation, distribution, and princess styleline samples and bring them to our next class meeting.
  • Choose one of the half-scale manipulations to cut and sew from muslin fabric.
  • Complete full-scale Size 8 Womenswear slopers and bring them to our next class meeting.
